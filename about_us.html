Our Heritage of Excellence

Our Journey Through Time

Discover the milestones that shaped Kwality Walls into the premium ice cream brand loved by millions across India.

1970

The Beginning

Founded with a vision to create premium ice cream that brings families together. Started with just three flavors and an unwavering commitment to quality.

1985

National Expansion

Expanded across major Indian cities, introducing innovative flavors like Alphonso Mango and establishing our signature quality standards.

1995

Innovation Era

Launched the iconic Cornetto series and introduced advanced freezing technology to maintain perfect texture and taste.

2010

Premium Revolution

Launched premium artisanal range with exotic flavors and sustainable packaging, setting new industry standards for quality.

2025

Digital Future

Leading the digital transformation with innovative flavors, sustainable practices, and personalized experiences for the modern consumer.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about our products, orders, and services.

Our premium quality comes from using only the finest ingredients sourced globally, traditional craftsmanship methods, and rigorous quality control standards. We use real vanilla, authentic Alphonso mangoes, Belgian chocolate, and fresh dairy from trusted farms to create our distinctive flavors.

Yes, all our ice cream products are 100% vegetarian. We use only vegetarian-friendly ingredients and stabilizers. Each product is clearly marked with the green dot symbol indicating vegetarian status as per Indian food regulations.

For bulk orders (50+ units), please use our contact form above and select "Bulk Orders" as the inquiry type. Provide details about quantity, flavors, and delivery requirements. Our business team will contact you within 24 hours with pricing and availability.

For special events and parties, we accept orders starting from 25 units. We offer special event packaging and can provide dry ice for transportation. Contact our events team for customized solutions and pricing.

Currently, our products are available through authorized retailers and online delivery partners in major cities. Use our store locator to find the nearest retailer or check availability on popular delivery apps like Swiggy, Zomato, and BigBasket.

We maintain strict cold chain management from production to retail. Our products are manufactured in ISO-certified facilities, undergo rigorous quality testing, and are stored at optimal temperatures throughout the supply chain. Each batch is tracked for quality assurance.

We're always looking for quality retail partners. Please contact our business development team through the form above, selecting "Business Partnership" as your inquiry type. Include details about your location, store type, and refrigeration capabilities.
